# Privacy Policy for Flare

**Effective Date:** November 5, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 5, 2025

## Introduction

Flare ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Flare mobile application (the "App"). By using Flare, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- **Name:** We collect your full name for account creation and to display to other users.
- **Email Address:** We collect your email address for account creation, authentication, and communication purposes.
- **Username:** We collect your chosen username to uniquely identify your account and enable other users to find you.
- **Phone Number (Optional):** If you choose to verify your phone number, it is encrypted on your device and stored in an unreadable, hashed format. We use this solely for contact-based friend discovery.

**Profile Information:**
- **Profile Picture:** You may choose to upload a profile picture. We store only the photos you explicitly choose to upload.
- **Bio:** Any biographical information you choose to add to your profile.

**User-Generated Content:**
- **Flares:** When you create a flare (event), we collect the flare name, description, activity type, time, and any photos or GIFs you attach. **Important: All flare data is automatically deleted after 30 days.**
- **Comments:** Any comments you post on flares are stored in our database. Comments are deleted when the associated flare is deleted (after 30 days).

### 1.2 Location Information

**Location Data is NOT Stored:**
- We **do not store** your location data on our servers in any capacity.
- Location information is used **only locally on your device** to display nearby flares on the map.
- When you create a flare, you manually select a location for that specific event. This location is associated with the flare, not with your account.
- Location services are only used when you actively use the map feature and can be disabled at any time in your device settings.

### 1.3 Photos and Media

- We store **only photos you explicitly choose to upload** to the App.
- Photos may be used for:
  - Profile pictures
  - Flare event images
- You maintain full control over your uploaded photos and can delete them at any time.
- Photos associated with flares are automatically deleted after 30 days when the flare expires.

### 1.4 Usage and Analytics Data

We collect limited analytics data to improve the App:
- **App Usage Patterns:** Which features you use and how often.
- **Crash Reports:** Technical information about app crashes to help us fix bugs.
- **Performance Metrics:** App loading times and performance data.
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version.

This data is collected through Firebase Analytics and is used solely to improve app performance and user experience.

### 1.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** track your location continuously.
- We do **not** access your photo library without permission.
- We do **not** read your contacts unless you explicitly grant permission.
- We do **not** collect payment information (the App is free).

## 3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

### 3.1 Data Storage

- **Account Data:** Stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure until you delete your account.
- **Flare Data:** Automatically deleted after 30 days.
- **Phone Numbers:** Encrypted on your device using SHA-256 hashing before storage. Stored in hashed format only.
- **Photos:** Stored in Firebase Cloud Storage with secure access controls.

### 3.2 Data Retention

- **Account Information:** Retained until you delete your account.
- **Flares and Comments:** Automatically deleted after 30 days from flare creation.
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for up to 14 months in aggregate, anonymized form.

### 3.3 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication through Firebase Authentication
- Phone number hashing with SHA-256
- Secure access controls on databases
- Regular security updates

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Update Your Information

You can access and update your information at any time through the App settings:
- Edit your name, bio, and profile picture
- Change your email or password
- Update notification preferences

### 5.2 Delete Your Account

You have the right to delete your account at any time:
- Go to **Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Safety ‚Üí Delete Account**
- All your data will be permanently deleted, including:
  - Account information (name, email, username)
  - Profile picture and bio
  - Friends and group memberships
  - Flares you created
  - Comments you posted

**Account deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.**

### 5.3 Control Location Services

You can enable or disable location services at any time:
- In iOS Settings ‚Üí Flare ‚Üí Location
- Choose "Never," "While Using the App," or "Always"
- The App works with location services disabled, but you won't see nearby flares on the map

### 5.4 Manage Push Notifications

You can control push notifications:
- In the App: Settings ‚Üí Notifications
- In iOS Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Flare

### 5.5 Contact Discovery

You can choose whether to sync contacts for friend discovery:
- In the App: Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Safety
- Disable contact sync to stop contact-based friend suggestions
